Question 4:What is the URL for the full GetCapabilities request to the service?
What is the Name of the service?
What Format(s) are available for GetMap requests from the service?
How many layers are included in the service? Make sure to include all containing elements as part of your count.

Answer (Service 1):

Name: Global Fire Information Management System (GFIMS).

http://geonetwork4.fao.org/wms/wms.php?SERVICE=WMS&VERSION=1.1.1&REQUEST=GETCAPABILITIES&BBOX

Formats: According to the metadata yielded by the GetCapabilities URL, the formats rendered include .png, MODIS data in .jpg and .tif formats, shapefiles (.shp), text (.txt) files, .vnd.wap.wbmp, .kml, WMS version 1.1.1, email, and .csv formats.
Layers: There are three layers available: GFIMS WMS Service, Fires - Past 48 Hours, and Fires - Past 24 hours.
