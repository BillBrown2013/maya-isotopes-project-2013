Caries: A "Disease Process"

FALL 2013 GEOGRAPHY 591 PROBLEMS CLASS
PROJECT DESCRIPTION:


My goal for this project is to create an online GIS modeling the spatial distribution of rates of caries (dental cavities), porotic hyperostosis, ratios of the stable isotopes 13C/12C and 15N/14N, and unstable isotope ratios of Sr/Rb in the Classic Maya world.
